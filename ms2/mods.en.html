Enable 10-Player Dungeon Meter in all dungeons

This enables the chaos dungeon meter to be working in all dungeons, this shows the damage dealt, damage taken, crit rate, etc. of your party members.

The script is a PowerShell script, save it with the ".ps1" extension. Inside the script the first 4 paths can be edited to match your setup like stated in the script comments.
